How to Join a Reward Program?

To join a Reward Program offered by our Merchant is easy, just tap on “More” to look or search for the Reward Program that you like to join and subscribed it. To store your existing Reward Card detail to goREWARD App is equally easy, just tap on “More”, look for the Reward Program, enter your detail, scan or enter your existing card number to complete. You never forget or need to carry the plastic cards anymore!


How to view Latest Promotion and Update?

Just tap on your Reward Program, you can view the latest Promotions, exciting Deals and Update anytime, anywhere.


How to get latest direct marketing brochure

Once you have subscribe to a specific merchant card, you be able to view the latest promo offered by the merchant. If there are certain promo you do not wish to see, simply unsubscribe the merchant card within "Card" section by selecting the specific merchant that offer the unwanted promo. .
